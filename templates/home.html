Ordered List, Unordered List and Definition List example,

Ordered List:

<OL(orderedlist)><LI(listitems)></LI></OL> Unordered List: <UL(unorderedlist)><LI(listitems)></LI></UL> Definition List: <DL(definitionlist)><DT(DEFINITIONtERM)></DT><DD(Definition)></DD></DL>

Anchor Tag:

<a href="http://www.source.gov"> <a href="website source"><img src="imageSource.jpeg" alt="image not supported" target="_blank/_self/_parent/_top"></a>

Video and Audio Elements:

<video height="300" width="480" autoplay src="myVideo.mp4">Video not supported</video> <audio height="300" width="480" autoplay src="MySong.mp3">Video not supported</audio>

IFrame:

<iframe src="frame source" title="description" height="100" width="200"></iframe>

Imagemap:

<img src="--" alt="image not supported" usemap="map"> <map name="map"><area shape="rect" coords="34,44,270,350" alt="smiley" href="mapped.htm"></map>
